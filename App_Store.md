## Privacy Policy - History Talk

**History Talk** is a free app developed by **Sentix Apps**. This SERVICE is provided by Sentix Apps at no cost and is intended for use as is.

### 1. Information Collection and Use

**History Talk** does not collect or store any **personal information** (such as name, email address, or phone number) directly on our own servers.

However, the app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services include:

* **Google AdMob:** Used for delivering advertisements. On iOS, this may access your **IDFA** (Identifier for Advertisers) if you provide permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt.
* **Firebase Analytics:** Used to collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the app.
* **Apple Services:** Information related to your Apple ID for app functionality and performance.

### 2. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

In compliance with Apple’s privacy standards, if you are using this app on an iOS device, you will be asked for permission to track your activity across other companies’ apps and websites for advertising purposes. We use this data solely to provide more relevant advertisements through **Google AdMob**. You can opt-out at any time through your device's "Privacy & Security" settings.

### 3. Log Data

In case of an error or crash while using the app, we collect data known as **Log Data** through third-party products (like Firebase Crashlytics). This data may include your device’s IP address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app at the time of the error, and other statistics. This is used strictly for debugging and performance optimization.

### 4. Cookies

This app does not explicitly use cookies. However, third-party services (such as advertisers) may use cookies or similar technologies to improve their services and deliver personalized content.

### 5. Data Retention and Deletion

Since we do not collect personal identifiers directly, we do not store personal data. Any anonymous analytical data is retained by the third-party providers (Google/Apple) according to their respective retention policies.

### 6. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

### 7. Security

We value your trust. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect any data processed by the app, remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

### 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. All updates will be posted on this page and are effective immediately.
